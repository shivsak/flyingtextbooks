About Flying Textbooks


As a student in college, I know that Textbooks are expensive. And it's hard to find cheap textbooks. It's even harder to sell your old textbooks. Currently, the way I would try to buy or sell used books is through a Facebook page.

But Facebook posts often get overlooked because of all the posts about other stuff and the non-standardized posting format. Which means that a lot of my books just lay around collecting dust and taking up a lot of valuable dorm room space.

Hence, flying textbooks.

If you want to buy books, you can search for books by Title, Author, ISBN or Class Number to find the book you need.

If you want to sell a book, simply click sell, and enter the book's information. (There's no login or signup or anything). Listing expire after a month, or you can remove them by marking it as sold.

That's it!
